About Us


The Lisa Ross Birth and Women's Center is a nonprofit, freestanding birth center near downtown Knoxville, TN. Our goal is to provide a unique system of women's health care to promote wellness, personal empowerment, and family involvement in the birth process and in women's health in general.

We embody the Birth Center concept, designed to provide access to superior quality, highly-personalized, and lower cost care.
